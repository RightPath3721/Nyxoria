Questions

Before you cross over.

Is this a real AI or a scripted bot?

Nyxoria runs on Claude 3.5 Sonnet (with optional Grok backend), wrapped in a custom persona layer that keeps her voice consistent across every conversation.

Is the card pull random?

Yes — drawn from a 78-card deck using a cryptographically seeded shuffle. The oracle then interprets the card in the context of your ongoing reading.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Anytime, in two clicks. Stripe handles all billing. No emails, no friction.

Will my conversations stay private?

All readings are encrypted at rest. We never train models on your data. You may delete any reading permanently from your journal.

Is this medical or psychological advice?

No. Nyxoria is for reflection and entertainment. If you are in crisis, please reach out to a qualified professional.
